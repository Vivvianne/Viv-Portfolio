About Myself

My name is Vivvianne Kimani and I love creating solutions using code. Solutions that improve peoples’ quality of life and make their life experiences worth the while. I got curious about coding by observing 2 individuals; Chloe O’brien and Careena Flora. These 2 ladies inspired me in 2 different seasons of my life. Careena, my sister, was invited to study computer science and mathematics when I was just joining high school and she got me curious about computers. Then Watching Chloe O’brien a fictional character in 24 the series doing stuff using code introduced me into the world of code. The fact that she was female and good in what she did was even more inspiring.

I didn't get to pursue software engineering on campus, I was picked for a different course. and ended up pursuing a masters in Environmental Management because by this time, my priorities had changed. I also had children during this period and got thinking again about software engineering. I researched on schools and I came across Moringa school. It was one of the most craziest, most stressful 6 months I have had in a long time and at the same time, most satisfying and rewarding. I did the full stack option and got to learn how to build applications. I built an application that helps one get information about their mental health if they suspect to be going through depression and test themselves to know the severity. I was also part of a team of 4 that was able to build a data analytics application for a client that will help in their company in creating good reports for the purposes of understanding how they are doing and forecasting their future.
